Audio examples of the five listening conditions referenced in the manuscript, illustrating the settings used in the experiment.

Mono

< short description of this condition -->

A single-speaker narration (male or female, randomly assigned).

Diotic

< short description of this condition -->

Male and female speech narratives were arithmetically summed and the resulting mixture presented identically to both ears.

Dichotic — Same Gender

< short description of this condition -->

Two concurrent speech streams of the same gender (male–male or female–female) presented separately to the left and right ears.

Dichotic — Different Gender

< short description of this condition -->

Two concurrent speech streams of the different gender (A male and a female) presented simultaneously to separate ears.

Switch

Male and female speech narratives were arithmetically summed and the resulting mixture presented identically to both ears.
